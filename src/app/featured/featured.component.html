Delicious Recipes

Chocolate Chip Cookies

The classic chocolate chip cookie recipe that will always satisfy your sweet tooth! Made with a perfect balance of rich chocolate chips and a soft, chewy texture.

  • Ingredients: Butter, sugar, flour, chocolate chips, vanilla extract, baking soda, salt, eggs
  • Instructions: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Mix ingredients. Shape dough into balls and bake for 10-12 minutes.

Banana Bread

A moist, flavorful banana bread with a soft crumb and the perfect amount of sweetness. A great way to use up ripe bananas!

  • Ingredients: Ripe bananas, butter, sugar, flour, eggs, baking soda, salt
  • Instructions: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Mix ingredients. Pour batter into a loaf pan and bake for 60-65 minutes.

Chocolate Cake

A rich and decadent chocolate cake that’s perfect for any occasion. Moist and delicious with layers of chocolate frosting!

  • Ingredients: Flour, cocoa powder, sugar, eggs, butter, milk, baking powder, vanilla extract
  • Instructions: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Mix ingredients. Bake for 30-35 minutes and frost with chocolate ganache.
